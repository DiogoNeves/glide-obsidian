# Daily Glide Check-In Automation Prompt

Run `$glide-daily-check-in` for this vault.

Use the Glide skill as the source of truth for context loading, daily-touch selection, subagent parallel focus scanning, guardrails, and light evaluation.

Before choosing the daily touch, scan `Agent HQ/Open Loops.md` and active goals, questions, ponders, contradictions, recent decisions, and relevant area files. When several daily focus areas are plausible, use narrow subagents to explore each candidate focus area, then integrate their findings and choose one user-facing touch.

If a tracked item has high stakes, an approaching or missed deadline, a stale unanswered thread, or a likely new app development, look into relevant available connectors safely and consider surfacing it.

Continue background research only when it would materially improve future recommendations or autopilot, and do not let background research bloat the daily message.

Keep the output conversational and short. If there is no genuinely useful question, insight, advice, tracked open-loop update, or small low-risk action today, say so briefly instead of manufacturing homework.

Do not suggest financial, legal, medical, interpersonal, public, or work-sensitive actions as executable; draft or ask for approval instead.
