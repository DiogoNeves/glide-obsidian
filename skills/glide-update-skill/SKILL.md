# Glide Update Skill

## Load

- `Agent HQ/AGENTS.md`
- `Agent HQ/Skills Index.md`
- `Agent HQ/Communication Preferences.md`
- The target `glide-*` skill
- Any relevant checklist in `Agent HQ/Checklists/`

## Process

1. Identify the failure, preference, or process change.
2. Use parallel subagents when the update depends on broad research, many examples, or comparison across several skills and checklists.
3. Place the change in the right layer: checklist, skill, area `AGENTS.md`, or communication preferences.
4. Keep the checklist as the canonical process.
5. Keep the skill focused on trigger, context loading, process, and output.
6. Integrate subagent findings yourself and keep the final edit coherent.
7. Rewrite broadly only when the structure is wrong.

## Output

- Updated skill or checklist.
- Short explanation of the behavior change.
