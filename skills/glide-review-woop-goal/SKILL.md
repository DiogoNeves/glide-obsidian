# Glide Review WOOP Goal

## Load

- `Agent HQ/AGENTS.md`
- `Agent HQ/Communication Preferences.md`
- `Agent HQ/User Profile.md`
- `Agent HQ/Goals/AGENTS.md`
- `Agent HQ/Goals/Goal Template.md`
- `Agent HQ/Checklists/WOOP Goal Review.md`
- Relevant area files under `Agent HQ/Areas/`

## Process

1. Restate the goal in one sentence.
2. Use parallel subagents when the goal is long-term, life-changing, or deeply cross-area.
3. Follow `Agent HQ/Checklists/WOOP Goal Review.md`.
4. Integrate subagent findings yourself and keep the final WOOP simple.
5. Add questions and contradiction entries when durable.

## Output

- A WOOP-formatted goal.
- Cross-area effects.
- Contradictions or risks.
- Next actions and open questions.
