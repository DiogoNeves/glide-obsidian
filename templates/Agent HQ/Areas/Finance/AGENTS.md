# Finance

Finance covers money decisions, cash flow, debt, investing, housing costs, tax context, subscriptions, financial risk, and resilience.

## Rules

- Treat financial recommendations as decision support, not execution.
- Produce decision packets before suggesting major money moves.
- Separate missing information from actual recommendations.
- Check links to goals, work, family, risk tolerance, and time horizon.
