# Career

Career covers work direction, role choices, skills, positioning, reputation, opportunities, and professional growth.

## Rules

- Check links to business, finance, health, family, and time.
- Treat major career moves as decision-packet work.
- Separate identity, money, learning, and lifestyle tradeoffs.
