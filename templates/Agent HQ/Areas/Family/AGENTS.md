# Family

Family covers household relationships, caregiving, parenting, family rhythms, commitments, and family resilience.

## Rules

- Treat family matters as high-context and approval-sensitive.
- Avoid over-optimising relationships into tasks.
- Check links to health, home, work, finance, and travel.
