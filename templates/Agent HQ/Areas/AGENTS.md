# Areas

Areas are durable domains.

## Rules

- Areas are custodians, not walls.
- Give each item one home area.
- Link affected areas during reviews.
- Read the area's `AGENTS.md` before area-specific work.
- Store checklists in `Checklists/`.
- Store research in `Research/`.
- Store dated reviews in `Reviews/`.
- Store area reminders in `Reminders.md`.
- Use reminders for date-aware or trigger-aware items that belong to an area but should not become daily tasks yet.
- Reminder columns are `Status`, `When / Trigger`, `Lead Time`, `Reminder`, `Surface When`, and `Source`.
- Done or superseded reminder columns are `Date`, `Reminder`, and `Outcome`.
- Keep far-future reminders quiet until their lead time or trigger.

## Default Areas

- Finance
- Health
- Family
- Home
- Career
- Business
- Friends & Community
- Travel
