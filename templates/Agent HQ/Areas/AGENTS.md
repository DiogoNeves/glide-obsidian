# Areas

Areas are durable domains.

## Rules

- Areas are custodians, not walls.
- Give each item one home area.
- Link affected areas during reviews.
- Read the area's `AGENTS.md` before area-specific work.
- Store checklists in `Checklists/`.
- Store research in `Research/`.
- Store dated reviews in `Reviews/`.

## Default Areas

- Finance
- Health
- Family
- Home
- Career
- Business
- Friends & Community
- Travel
