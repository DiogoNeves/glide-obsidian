# Research Instructions

Research supports goals, areas, and decisions over time.

## Defaults

- Start with the question, decision, or goal the research serves.
- Keep sources and claims separate.
- Use lived experience for patterns, language, edge cases, and failure modes.
- Validate important claims with stronger sources: papers, systematic reviews, credible books, official docs, primary data, or domain specialists.
- Track evidence quality, uncertainty, and dissent.
- Prefer syntheses, opportunity maps, and next experiments over endless collection.
- Draft outreach or public material for approval before use.

## Research Outputs

- Update `Research Index.md` for durable research.
- Store notes in `Agent HQ/Research/` or the relevant area `Research/` folder.
- Include source links and evidence type.
